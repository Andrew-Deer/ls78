N O U N S

Euransi(an) nouns don't distinguish a grammatical gender, and articles are used very seldom, in this international constructed language.
There are endings -n & -ho for all substantives & adjectives, and possessive particle -i or -yi. They are used almost only with a possessive
pronoun - never instead of the genitive particle e. The case particles are very close to prepositions, and they are used separately.
