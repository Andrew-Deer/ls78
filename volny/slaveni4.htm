A D V E R B S

  ADVERBIAL ENDING POSITIVE KOMPARATIVE SUPERLATIVE
well o devro devreno najdevreno
newly o nevo neveno najneveno

There are two groups of adverbs in Slaveni: The regular ones always end with o (being created from adjectives: nevyn = new nevo = newly), irregular adverbs mostly end with a or any other vowel and consonant (very seldom). We can cautiously compare both adverbial groups: But those irregular adverbs only with auxiliary adverbs vise (= more) and najvise (= the most) or in descending order: mene (= less) and najmene (= the least).
