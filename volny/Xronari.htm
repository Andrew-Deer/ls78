Xronari is the newest constructed language designed by Libor Sztemon. The word ,xronar‘ means a ,time‘, and Xronari would like to become the all time world language. ,Lingva Xronari‘ would like to compete with English... Its grammar is easy and relatively regular, but not so affected as in case of Esperanto and its imitations, and it lacks any diacritics. And there is one more adventage: Xronare lexical roots are partically inspired by a great number of various languages, including extinct ones, mostly from our Indo-European group.

‚Lingva Xronari‘ knows eight vowel qualities. Almost all of them can be either short or long. There are five diphthongs ai, au, ei, eu, ia and ie. The u always has to keep its full sound, but ‚i‘ is much finer in comparison with the common ‚i‘ - rather it just makes ‚a‘ or ‚e‘ longer and coloured [ai ei] - and in ‚ia‘ & ‚ie‘ sounds like [iya] & [iye], same like e.g. in Latin.
